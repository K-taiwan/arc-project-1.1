About

The whole concept of this app it to ingeniously link people that need to rent a vehicle prefereably(convertible) cruising vehicles, but we also cater to the ones that need fuel consumption, minivan, trucks. We cater to everyone, no need for any type of discrimination as long as we get paid. No need to signup or login. Just book the vehicle you want and pay up front. We will handle the rest. User friendly and simple logic of 1 day rent vehicle of 24hr's.
